Firmiana1.0 by ECNU Bioinformatic Lab

Firmiana is an important part of Galaxy/Firmiana platform which is a "one-stop" data analysis platform for proteome data, composed of a series of analysis tools for MS(mass spectra) data from experimental design to statistical analysis and further bioinformatics mining technique, such as peptide identification, peptide quantification, protein quantification, mainly including two divs: Experiment Management and Analysis Results Display.

Experiment Management

The Experiment Management div contains two aspects:
(1) The display of experiment, sample, reagent information;
(2) The addition and edition of experiment, sample, reagent information.

Analysis Results Display

The Analysis Results Display div contains two aspects:
(1) The display of analysis results for files from individual experiment;
(2) The display of analysis results for comparison study from multiple experiments.
