Welcome to Firmiana





 Firmiana is a "one-stop" data analysis platform for proteomics data, composed of a series of analysis tools for Mass Spectra data from experimental design to statistical analysis and further bioinformatics data-mining technique, such as peptide identification, peptide quantification, protein quantification. Meanwhile, this platform can also be used to create flexible proteome analysis workflow.
